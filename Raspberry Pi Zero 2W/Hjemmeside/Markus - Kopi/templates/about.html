Om IoT platformen

Project Overview

This IoT platform was developed as a first-year university project to demonstrate the integration of embedded systems with web technologies.

Our system collects data from three ESP32 microcontrollers equipped with various sensors, processes this data on a Raspberry Pi Zero 2W, and presents it through this intuitive web interface.

The platform allows for real-time monitoring of environmental conditions and will eventually support device control capabilities.
